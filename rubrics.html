Rules

Animation

Technique (14) Articulation (12) Creativity (10) Execution (14)
Every vocalist demonstrates excellent breath control, phrases are complete with no gasps for air. Notes are correctly supported and sustained, and there is little to no evidence of straining. Vocals are clear with little to no slurring/mumbling. Pronunciation is fairly accurate
- any mistakes are minor and do not negatively affect the performance. 		The vocalists make excellent use of harmonies and adlibs to enhance the entry
- some originality is shown in the arrangement.
The group has also been creative in terms of scripting, using interesting vocal combinations and blends that enhance the entry. 		Each vocalist sounds confident in their performance. Vocalists make appropriate use of dynamics to make the entry interesting. The energy in the vocals suits the chosen song, and vocalists convey emotion well. Vocal tones are consistent throughout the entry, and flair is shown through stylish elements (e.g. vocal runs)

Mix

Tuning (10) Timing (10) Volume Balance (10) Depth (10) Frequency Balance (10)
Notes were properly tuned. Tuning techniques are compatible with the style of song and fit with the overall mixing. Vocalists are properly timed with the original rhythm of the song. Group vocals are timed and cohesive. Instrumentals and vocals are well balanced and showcases a smooth volume spectrum while being dynamic.
All individual vocal tracks are leveled with each other and there are no peaks. The track is mastered well and the RMS level is compatible with various streaming platforms. 		The mix has depth without being dry. Reverbs, delays, and panning tools are well utilized and fills the stereo field without any clashing with the main track. These effects are used creatively. There is balance between the vocals and the instrumental in the EQ spectrum. The vocals can be clearly heard and the blend between the vocals are well done and balanced with each other. The tracks are cleaned well and the characteristics of the mix match with the style of the song with the general sound shaping in the mastering chain.

Art

Aesthetics (6) Creativity(10) Polish/Finish (10) Cohesion (12) Technique (12)
How well the art matches the song chosen and theme interpretation. Intentionality of the drawing. Choices made are unique and not overly repetitive. There are elements that memorably separate them from common, often-seen designs/poses. Cleanliness of the drawing; art is consistent both in style and quality, and care is put to ensure as such. Overall visual harmony of art in the context of a music video (collective posing of sprites/ outfit/design/color cohesion, etc.) Understanding and effective use of fundamental art concepts such as anatomy, lighting, color theory, etc. reflected on stylistic choices.
