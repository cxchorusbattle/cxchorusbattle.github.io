Delilah Wright

Client

There are things far more valuable than jewels… like me.

Age: 34
Pronouns: She/Her
Occupation: Widowed Heiress
Blood Type: O

Delilah was once married to Mr. Holmes, who had died in an unfortunate accident, where she was the sole inheritor. As the heiress of one of the largest companies, her haughty and arrogant demeanor matched her lavish lifestyle. However, there may be more to her underneath that cruel personality and that pearl necklace.

Tomas Walker

Suspect 1

No matter my opinion of Ms. Wright, to me the work is manageable and worth every penny.

Age: 28
Pronouns: He/Him
Occupation: Legal Aid
Blood Type: B

As Delilah Wright’s assistant, piles of paperwork would always seem to find its way to Tomas’s desk. Managing her large estate and money was no easy task but through his diligent work ethic and great legal sense he was considered one of Wright’s best workers. After all, allowing a worker to deal a large sum of money would take a lot of trust, wouldn’t it? Even so, Tomas’s relationship with Delilah seems estranged.

Scarlett Young

Suspect 2

Miss Delilah feels like an older sister more than a boss! There is no one she trusts more than me.

Age: 26
Pronouns: She/Her
Occupation: Maid
Blood Type: A

Scarlett works as Delilah Wright’s maid and personal assistant, assisting her with day to day tasks. The young and shy girl mysteriously appeared for the position of personal assistant right after Mr. Holmes’s death. She was brought to assist Wright in her preparation for the grand opening event of the cruise and is one of Wright’s most trusted personnel.

Alistair Cox

Suspect 3

Snap shots can capture the truths within a hidden narrative.

Age: 24
Pronouns: He/Him
Occupation: Photographer
Blood Type: AB

A member of the press, Alistair spends his time taking pictures and collecting commentary from around the ship. Alistair is always caught with a smile but his demeanor is hard to read. His purpose on the ship is to shadow Delilah Wright for an exposè on her history, but his shallow questions and carefree attitude hints that he may have different intentions.

Leigh Moriarty

Shopkeep

Anything can be sold for the right price.

Age: 27
Pronouns: Any
Occupation: Casino Dealer / Shopkeeper
Blood Type: AB

From the many amenities on the cruise, the shopkeeper and casino dealer Leigh seems to be the most attention grabbing. As a worker on the ship, Leigh acts as the eyes on the ship, observing things that may seem obvious but are hidden to carefree vacation goers. To Leigh, everything is available for sale-- even information.

Frederick Jackson

Detective

The answer is always insignificant to the story behind it.

Age: 39
Pronouns: He/Him
Occupation: Detective
Blood Type: B

A hardboiled detective, Frederick seems unreliable at times but is a talented detective who knows how to keep information confidential. He was a long-time friend and relation to Delilah, who leaned on him for advice and other matters. Something caused a rift between them and led to bad blood and being unable to be in the same room as one another. Through that stoic demeanor, Delilah can still be seen lingering in his mind. Frederick is on this cruise for an unknown case that he was assigned.
