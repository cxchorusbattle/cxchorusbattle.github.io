Rules

Group Requirements

  • Each team must have a minimum of 3 members, and may have up to a maximum of 14 members.
  • Team composition requirements are as follows:
    • Minimum 3 vocalists, with each vocalist having a “fair” amount of lines based on the number of vocalists in the team.
    • There is no limit to the number of vocalists or staff, as long as the team total is under 14 members.
    • Doubles (those who are participating in a vocalist and staff position) will be counted as one member.
  • Each member is only allowed to be a part of one team at a time.
  • Members of eliminated teams may join as members of other teams in future rounds.
  • Anonymous members are allowed. However, the CB staff must be informed of their identities.
  • Every team must have 2 points of contact, a team leader and backup leader.
  • Each member must have a public social media account. (Twitter, Youtube, etc.)
  • Teams may be freely changed during the registration period. Please notify CB organizers of any changes.
  • Blacklisted individuals, or members of the community who have a significant number of allegations with evidence to back it up, will be blocked from registering and participating in this Chorus Battle. Please note that any teams that attempt to register with these individuals will be informed when submitting their registration.
  • In the case that these individuals attempt to hide their identity and register under a different alias, please inform the CB staff. Teams that partake in this action of deception will be immediately disqualified and their entries removed from any official CB playlist. We take the safety of the individuals in our community seriously and hope for your cooperation in making this event safe and enjoyable.

Entry Requirements

  • The entry length must be between 2:30 - 5:30 minutes. Entries longer than 5:30 minutes will not be judged passed the 5:30 minute mark.
  • All languages, styles, and genres of music are permitted
  • Translyrics, remixes, and mash-ups are permitted with proper credit of the original artist.
    • Lack of credits will results in heavy point deductions.
  • Original songs are permitted.

Submission Requirements

  • Entry title format: 【 #CXCB-A1】 Song Title 【Team Name】
  • All entries must be submitted via google forms that will be provided 1 week before the entry deadline.
  • Any video submission format is allowed as long as it is accessible by the judges
  • The following links must be submitted through the form:
    • Entry Link
    • Theme Interpretation
    • Script
    • English Translation (If the song is not in English)
    • Audio Folder (Mix and Acapella in .wav format)
    • Art Folder (All drawn art/assets)
    • Third Party Assets
    • Alternative video link
  • All proper credits must be provided in the description of your video
    • Vocal/Staff Credits
    • Original Song Credits
    • Third Party Assets
  • Submission links are NOT required to be in the description (apart from third party assets)
  • Any improper sources or credits will result in heavy point deductions

Grace Period

  • The grace period lasts for 24 hours after the end of a round.
  • 1 point will be deducted for every hour into the grace period for the first 12 hours
  • 1.5 points will be deducted for every hour for the last 12 hours.
  • Items can be used to waive point deductions in the grace period (Camera & Binoculars)
  • Entries submitted after the grace period is over will be disqualified
  • Render-based extensions are permitted for render-based issues (abnormally long render time or persistent render issues) by providing the team name and screenshot of render to the organizers through Twitter DM.

FAQ

Why do we need a team leader?

A team leader serves as your main point of contact to us in case you have any questions. This helps us keep the DMs organized so we appreciate your cooperation! Any of the members are free to message us as well as long as it is just one person from your team!

How are theme interpretation scores given?

This will be graded in two parts: the written theme interpretation and then the points earned from using the evidence provided in each round. More information can be found on the ‘Scores’ page!

Will I lose points for theme interpretation if our team’s theory on what happened is wrong?

Absolutely not! As long as you can sell your theory well you’re good to go.

Can I use the official character sprites and CB logo?

You may use the CB logos freely as long as you credit dodo @415x2 in your asset link and video description. Use of official character sprites are NOT allowed.

Is there a limit to how many members we can change after the first round?

You are only limited by points you have to purchase items in our shop. You can swap members as much as you want as long as you have the points to buy the member passes.

Why are some participants blacklisted?

Blacklisting certain participants that have been known to engage in harmful practices is something that the CB teams feels is necessary in order to maintain a fun and safe chorus battle environment. We do not take accusations lightly and will only blacklist individuals that have sufficient enough evidence to warrant doing so.
