The Story so Far...

Boarding!

You have been cordially invited to one of the hottest cruises, Cruise X!

Cruise X is a world-class trip through the beautiful waters of the Bahamas, pairing our amenities and entertainment with the gorgeous landscape. To celebrate our launch, we have a very special event planned for all of our passengers!

You, however, are onboard for a different reason. What will unfold during the event?

Book your tickets to find out!

Round 1

Night falls on the first night of the cruise and the special event that everyone's been waiting for is about to kick off -- the ball! The theme of the ball is "Masquerade" and every guest attending is required to wear a mask. Unlike the other guests, however, you're on cruise for a completely different reason-- to assist Frederick Jackson on a case. As you walk into the ball, you meet with Frederick as he debriefs the case one last time.

CLIENT: DELILAH WRIGHT
REQUEST: KEEP HER AND HER JEWELRY SAFE

Illustration by Tachi @tachii_xx
THEME: MASQUERADE


MAIN MISSION:

Wright believes there exist individuals aboard the ship that want to steal her sizable fortune. Find out who is the most suspicious and trail them. Make sure not to lose sight of them in the crowd.

Follow the theme of the video!

Major Evidence (Up to 5 points):

  • duplicity (2)
  • masks (2)
  • who’s who (3)
  • dancing in circles/repetition (3)
  • mirrored (1)


SIDE MISSIONS (Each worth 2 points):

Keep a note of your potential suspects.

Make sure you’re blending in.

Round 2

Delilah Wright is dead. In the wake, you find your senior detective, who tells you to pull everyone from the crowd back in the bloodied ballroom. The lights come back on, and the body is wheeled away for safekeeping and further inspection. You go through each and every one before you end up right where you started, with three suspects right before you. No one is innocent, after all, not even you. And the fact that you’re shaken is secondary to the fact that there’s a killer onboard. You have to find out who.

CLIENT: FREDERICK JACKSON
REQUEST: FIND OUT WHO KILLED DELILAH WRIGHT
THEME: RAISED STAKES

Raised Stakes Definition: increased costs, risks, or considerations involved in taking an action or reaching a conclusion

MAIN MISSION:

Your client has just died, and turned this story of thievery into a murder mystery. A body dead and three suspects on your hands — who could it be? You need to get to the bottom of this in any way possible.

Follow the theme of the video!

Major Evidence (Up to 5 points):

  • in triplicate(3x) (1)
  • pearls (1)
  • murder (2))
  • puppets (2)
  • theatre (2)
  • confusion (3)
  • fate (3)
  • make-believe (4)


SIDE MISSIONS (Each worth 2 points):

It has to be one of them. There is no other way.

Round 3

Delilah Wright is alive. With nobody, you find yourself alone in this mission to uncover the secret behind Delilah Wrights "death". Once you eliminate the impossible, whatever remains, no matter how improbable, must be the truth. But what if it was the truth you refused to face? What if it wasn’t an ending you wanted? What would you do if the only person you trusted was the one who had done it all? You swallow the bile that's rising from your throat and look for the culprit yourself.

DELILAH WRIGHT
REQUEST: FIND OUT WHO KILLED DELILAH WRIGHT
THEME: TURNABOUT

Turnabout Definition: a sudden and complete change or reversal of a situation.

MAIN MISSION:

Don’t panic. Stay calm and remember what you were put here to do. And remember… Once you eliminate the impossible, whatever remains, no matter how improbable, must be the truth. Follow the theme of the video!
